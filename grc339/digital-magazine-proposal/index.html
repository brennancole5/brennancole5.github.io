Introduction

Scrawl Magazine

Scrawl Magazine showcases the art that can’t be ignored. It’s where grit meets curation. We explore the spaces in between: alleyways and underpasses, buzzing tattoo shops, forgotten walls turned into canvases. This magazine highlights the many forms of public artwork, from murals and tattoos to graffiti, street art, and more.
